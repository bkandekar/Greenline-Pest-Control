Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 7, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Greenline Pest Control ("we," "our," or "us"). We represent a premier hygiene-first pest management service provider in Maharashtra. We are deeply committed to protecting your personal privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle, process, and respect personal data collected when you navigate or interact with our static website (https://greenlinepest.com/).

2. Zero Server-Side Storage Policy

We are a highly secure, offline-first client-side platform. Your data integrity and security are guaranteed under our strict zero server-side storage paradigms:

  • Simple Inquiry Form: Any information submitted through our local Contact Page inquiry form (Name, Phone Number, and Message) is processed entirely within your local browser. It does not transmit to, nor is it stored in, any database, server, or cloud storage system. Submitting this form simply displays an on-screen success confirmation and relies on our direct manual callback systems.
  • WhatsApp Booking Modal: When you utilize our "Book Pest Control" scheduler modal to request an appointment, the details you enter (Full Name, Mobile, Email, Service Needed, Address, Preferred Date/Time, and Message) are never stored on our website's hosting server or transmitted to any third-party marketing agency. Instead, our JavaScript engine compiles your text locally into a formatted message and redirects your browser to WhatsApp's official API link. You send the message yourself via WhatsApp directly to our support desk.

3. Cookies and Tracking

To keep your browsing experience clean and lightning-fast, we do not deploy aggressive marketing trackers, tracking pixels, or profiling cookies. We may use standard, lightweight local session objects solely to store preferences (such as dismissing the promotional top banner) to ensure you do not see repetitive banners during a single browsing session.

4. Third-Party Integrations

Our website features standard integrations with licensed third-party platforms to enhance convenience:

  • Google Maps Iframe: We embed an official Google Maps iframe on our Contact Page to show our physical HQ location on Garden Road. Google may collect standard technical parameters (such as your IP address or browser coordinates) when you view the map in accordance with their standard privacy policies.
  • WhatsApp Communication: Since our primary booking paradigm is routed through WhatsApp, clicking any booking buttons will redirect you to WhatsApp's official network. Their standard privacy rules and end-to-end encryption protocols apply to your chats.

5. User Rights and Data Access

Because we do not store your data on our servers, we do not maintain database records that can be modified, exported, or deleted. For any offline communication records established during our manual callbacks or WhatsApp conversations, you retain full rights to request that we delete your chat histories or contact numbers from our local business devices at any time.

6. Policy Updates

We may modify this Privacy Policy from time to time to align with changing regulations or corporate safety protocols. Any updates will be published directly on this page with a revised "Last Updated" timestamp.
