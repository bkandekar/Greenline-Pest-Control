Terms & Conditions

Last Updated: July 7, 2026

1. Acceptance of Terms

Welcome to Greenline Pest Control. By booking any residential, housing society, or commercial pest control services from us, you agree to be legally bound by these Terms and Conditions. Please read them thoroughly before confirming your booking with our desk.

2. Bookings, Time Slots, & Scheduling

All service requests scheduled via our website booking modal are preliminary and subject to manual slots confirmation. Our support desk will reach out to confirm a mutual booking window. While we make every attempt to arrive precisely within your preferred slot (e.g. Morning 9 AM - 12 PM), external factors like traffic congestion or on-site operational extensions may cause minor delays. We will communicate any schedule modifications proactively.

3. Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC) Rules

Our Standard and Premium AMC plans represent recurring quarterly service structures. Standard regulatory rules governing AMCs include:

  • Service Intervals: A standard annual contract comprises 4 mandatory scheduled visits spaced at approximate 90-day intervals. It is the responsibility of the property owner or resident to allow our technicians access during these scheduled periods.
  • Priority Warranty: AMC subscribers are entitled to complimentary emergency spot treatments for covered pests (e.g., cockroaches or ants) if recurring nesting is observed between standard quarterly visits.
  • Contract Rollovers: AMC contracts do not roll over automatically. Our team will contact you 30 days prior to contract expiry to process renewal terms.

4. Pre-Service Site Preparation Requirements

To ensure high efficacy and maximize chemical and physical barrier distribution, clients must adhere to our preparation guidelines:

  • Exposed Foodstuffs: All raw food items, kitchen containers, and dining utensils must be completely covered or placed inside secure cabinets prior to general spraying.
  • Pet & Child Safety: Kids, elderly residents, and domestic pets must be kept away from active treatment areas. For deep liquid misting or chemical fogging, they must vacate the premises during the treatment and for 1-2 hours afterward until all surfaces dry.
  • Access to Borders: Furniture, cabinets, and appliances should be moved 6-12 inches away from walls to provide our technicians with clear access to baseboards, crevices, and structural cracks.

5. Liability Limitations

Greenline Pest Control maintains rigorous quality and sterile standards. However, we note the following operational liability boundaries:

  • Pre-existing Structural Damage: We are not liable for any structural collapse, weakened wooden beams, or plaster damage occurring due to historically deep termite tunnelings or rodent burrowings discovered during our procedures.
  • Hidden Infestations: While our treatments are thorough, some deep-nested pest reservoirs (e.g., inside deep hollow wall cavities or under foundation concrete slabs) may require multiple targeted structural interventions over time to clear completely.
  • Client Preparation Failures: We hold no liability for reduced efficacy or chemical skin reactions if residents fail to follow the safe preparation guidelines listed in Section 4.

6. Payment Methods & Policies

All one-time service packages must be settled immediately upon completion of the active treatment. For AMC packages, 50% of the annual fee is payable upon the first service visit, with the remaining 50% settled upon the second scheduled quarterly visit. We accept UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), local check transfers, net banking, debit/credit cards, and cash. Corporate clients will be provided with formal GST compliant invoices with a standard 15-day credit cycle.

7. Cancellation & Rescheduling Policies

We respect your schedule and request that you respect ours. You can reschedule or cancel any confirmed booking free of charge up to 24 hours prior to your scheduled slot. Cancellations or changes made within 24 hours of the appointment may be subject to a minor mobilization fee of ₹200 to cover technician travel and operational booking costs.

These terms are governed by the local regulations and legal frameworks of Maharashtra, India. Any disputes arising from services rendered will be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the competent courts in Mumbai.
