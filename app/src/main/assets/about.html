Our Heritage

Dedicated to Healthy Environments

We prioritize your family's health, structural hygiene, and peace of mind by employing sterile, eco-conscious treatment paradigms.

The Greenline Story

Clean Homes, Healthy Living

Founded over a decade ago in Maharashtra, Greenline Pest Control set out to redefine an industry heavily reliant on strong, toxic chemicals. We recognized that homeowners and businesses deserved a more clinical, health-conscious alternative.

We focused our research on identifying biological gels, non-drift micro-emulsions, and low-toxicity insect growth regulators (IGRs) that actively target pests without polluting the indoor airspace or leaving aggressive odor profiles.

Today, we represent a premier choice for premium households, luxury buildings, hospitals, and food processing facilities across Sample Vihar. Our treatments are designed not just to kill, but to prevent, preserve, and protect.

To deliver highly effective, medical-grade pest control treatments while adhering strictly to child, pet, and senior-safe hygiene guidelines. We strive to sanitize, educate, and preserve standard environmental integrity in every space we enter.

Chronology

Our Growth & Milestones

A decade of continuous learning, certifications, and expansion across local neighborhoods.

2018

Safety Pledge

Our Clinical Safety Commitment

At Greenline Pest Control, we understand that we are guests in your home. Your children, parents, and beloved companion pets breathe the air we treat. Because of this, we strictly implement a Clean Safety Protocol on every visit:

  • Zero Odor Food-Grade Gels: Kitchen treatments use odorless, localized bio-gels that prevent aerosol pollution.
  • Child-Safe Demarcations: Bait and tracking configurations are securely placed inside tamper-resistant physical bays.
  • Pet-Friendly Spray Compounds: Liquid barriers utilize highly synthesized organic extracts that evaporate safely within minutes.
